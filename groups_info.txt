Groups

- Groups should get a new tab at the top.
- Groups are treated as users (@groupname)
- If you are in a group you can see mentions of that group.
- If you want to tweet to a group just mention it.

Group Pages
- Groups (/groups): Shows all tweets that mention the groups you've joined.
- Create Group (/groups/create): Allows you to register a new group name. 
- Find Group (/groups/discover): Lists off a bunch of groups to join. 
- Profile (/<groupname>): Shows group mentions
- Members (/<groupname>/members): Shows group members
